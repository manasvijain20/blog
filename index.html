places must visit

marine drive

I had visited marine drive in 10 October 2020.

This is a wonderful place to hangout, especially for those who like to take a walk by the coast. The recommended time would be at sunset. interesting part of Mumbai. There is no direct access to the water however pleasant place to relax or have a walk.A drive on Marine Drive in the evening affords great night vistas of Mumbai's skyline dotted with lights.. the walkway that adjoins the Sea makes for a pleasant walk and faces the Arabian Sea on one end, It's Awesome. One can spend whole night just enjoying cool breeze siting ideal right there..

The Princess Diaries
The Princess Diaries is a 2001 American coming-of-age teen comedy film produced by Walt Disney Pictures and directed by Garry Marshall.

This movie is about A clumsy, shy teenage girl who discovers that she is the princess of Genovia, a small European state. Thereafter, she must prove to her grandmother that she is worthy of her title. I love the movie and it is suitable for ages 12+.
